Privacy Policy

BC Free Flight — bcfreeflight.com — Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Overview

BC Free Flight ("we", "us", "our") operates the website bcfreeflight.com, which provides real-time weather conditions and live video for free-flight launch sites in British Columbia, Canada. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require user accounts or registration.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies of our own.
  • We do not log IP addresses beyond what is retained automatically by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages).

3. Third-Party Services

This site integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Weather Underground API — used to retrieve real-time weather station data. No personal data is sent to this service on your behalf.
  • YouTube (Google LLC) — used to embed a live video stream. YouTube may set cookies and collect usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN — used to serve Bootstrap CSS and JavaScript. jsDelivr may log requests in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — used to serve icon fonts. Google may log font requests in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical log data (such as IP addresses and browser user-agent strings) as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
